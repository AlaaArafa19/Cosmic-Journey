Cosmic Journey

Clouds Moon Mars Galaxy

The Clouds

Our journey begins here, leaving the ground behind and soaring through the beautiful blue sky and soft white clouds into the atmosphere.


The Moon

Entering the dark space, our first stop is the Moon. A quiet place full of craters, where human footsteps first marked the cosmic dust.


The Mars

Next, we reach Mars, the red desert of our solar system. Scientists dream of building the first human city under its rusty orange sky.


The Galaxy

Finally, we dive deep into the galaxy, swimming through sparkling nebulas, millions of stars, and the beautiful mysteries of the universe.
